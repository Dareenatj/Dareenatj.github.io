About Me

Hi! I'm Dareenat (Daisy) Jakdetchai, a second-year Physics Specialist and CS Minor student at the University of Toronto.

I’m passionate about the intersection of physics, biology, and computing. I don’t feel limited to one path — there’s so much I want to learn, and I believe that connecting ideas across disciplines is where real insight happens.

Outside of academics, I love painting, drawing, and collecting plants — especially cacti!
